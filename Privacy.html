Privacy and Policy

At HND CampSale, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

1. Information We Collect
  • Name, email address, and contact details when registering or contacting us

  • Cookies and usage data to improve site functionality
2. How We Use Your Data
  • Process orders and provide services
  • Improve user experience
  • Communicate updates and offers
  • Ensure site security and prevent fraud
3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Your information may only be shared with:

4. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences and optimize performance. You can manage cookie settings in your browser.

5. Data Security

Your data is stored securely with industry-standard encryption and protections. However, no online platform is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

To request data changes or removal, contact: support@hndcampsale.com

7. Third-Party Links

We are not responsible for privacy practices of third-party websites linked from our platform.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this policy without notice. The latest version will always be available on this page.
